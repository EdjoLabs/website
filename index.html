Powered by Artificial Intelligence and Blockchain Technology


Blockchain is a decentralised, distributed, immutable and transparent way to store information online. Content Protection uses blockchain to record content creator’s work on the network in order to ensure copyright protections are established, maintained and monitored.

Our Artificial Intelligence Web Crawler includes audio and image recognition. Using state-of-the-art machine learning algorithms, it is able to scan through the world-wide-web and report detected copyright infrignments back to the user.
